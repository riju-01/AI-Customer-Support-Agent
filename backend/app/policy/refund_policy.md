# Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2026

## 1. Overview

This Refund Policy governs all purchases made through ShopEase ("Company", "we", "our", or "us").

By placing an order through our website, customers acknowledge and agree to this Refund Policy.

---

## 2. All Sales Are Final

Unless expressly stated otherwise in this Refund Policy, all purchases are considered final.

Refunds, returns, exchanges, or cancellations will only be approved under the circumstances specifically described in this policy.

---

## 3. Eligible Refund Requests

A refund request may be considered only if:

* The customer receives a defective product.
* The customer receives a damaged product.
* The customer receives an incorrect product.
* The customer receives a product materially different from the product description.
* The product is confirmed lost during shipment.
* The product arrives with missing essential components required for normal use.

Approval of any refund request is subject to verification and compliance with this policy.

---

## 4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are strictly non-refundable:

* Digital products.
* Downloadable content.
* Software licenses.
* Gift cards.
* Store credits.
* Personalized or customized products.
* Made-to-order products.
* Clearance items.
* Final-sale items.
* Products showing signs of use, misuse, alteration, or damage caused by the customer.
* Products returned without original accessories or packaging where applicable.
* Requests submitted outside the eligibility period.

---

## 5. Refund Request Window

Customers must initiate a refund request within **7 calendar days** of the recorded delivery date.

Requests submitted after this period may be automatically rejected.

---

## 6. AI Customer Support Process

All refund, return, exchange, and cancellation requests must be initiated through the Company's AI Customer Support Agent.

The customer must provide any information requested by the AI Customer Support Agent, including:

* Order number
* Product details
* Description of the issue
* Supporting photographs
* Supporting videos
* Shipping information
* Any additional verification information requested

Failure to provide sufficient information may result in denial of the request.

---

## 7. Verification and Investigation

The Company reserves the right to verify all claims before approving any refund, return, exchange, or replacement.

Verification methods may include:

* Review of customer-provided evidence
* Shipping carrier verification
* Order history analysis
* Fraud detection systems
* Automated verification processes
* Human review when necessary

Providing false, misleading, manipulated, or fraudulent information may result in immediate rejection of the request.

---

## 8. Product Condition Requirements

To qualify for a return where applicable:

* The product must be in substantially the same condition as received.
* Original packaging should be retained where possible.
* All included accessories, manuals, and components must be returned.
* The product must not show evidence of misuse, abuse, modification, or negligence.

Failure to meet these requirements may result in denial of the refund request.

---

## 9. Return Shipping

Where a return is required:

* Customers may be required to return the product before a refund is processed.
* Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the Company determines that the issue resulted from Company error.
* Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless required by law.

The Company is not responsible for items lost during return transit.

---

## 10. Refund Approval

Approval of a refund request is not guaranteed.

Refund eligibility is determined solely based on:

* Compliance with this Refund Policy
* Verification of the reported issue
* Accuracy of information provided
* Fraud prevention and risk assessment procedures

The Company's determination shall be final to the extent permitted by applicable law.

---

## 11. Refund Method

Approved refunds will be issued only to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Refunds will not be issued through alternative payment methods except where required by law.

---

## 12. Refund Processing Time

Once approved, refunds may take between **7 and 15 business days** to appear in the customer's account.

Actual processing times may vary depending on:

* Payment provider
* Banking institution
* Credit card issuer
* Geographic location

The Company is not responsible for delays caused by third-party payment processors.

---

## 13. Order Cancellation

Orders may only be canceled before processing or shipment.

Once an order has entered fulfillment, packaging, or shipping stages, cancellation requests may be denied.

---

## 14. Exchanges and Replacements

The Company may, at its sole discretion, offer:

* Product replacement
* Product exchange
* Store credit
* Partial refund
* Full refund

The remedy provided will depend on the circumstances of the claim.

---

The Company reserves the right to deny any request that appears fraudulent, abusive, deceptive, or intended to exploit the refund process.

The Company may use:

* Artificial intelligence systems

* Risk scoring systems

Repeated abuse of refund privileges may result in:

* Account suspension

The Company reserves the right to submit evidence to financial institutions and payment processors in defense of disputed transactions.

## 21. Counterfeit, Fake, or Non-Authentic Product Claims

If a customer believes a product is counterfeit, fake, unauthorized, or not genuine, a claim may be submitted through the Company's AI Customer Support Agent.

The customer may be required to provide:

* Photographs of the product
* Packaging images
* Serial numbers
* Authentication reports
* Supporting documentation

The Company reserves the right to investigate such claims through automated systems, seller verification processes, manufacturer verification, or independent review.

If the claim is verified:

* A full refund may be issued.
* A replacement product may be provided.
* The seller may be subject to corrective action, suspension, or removal from the platform.

If the claim cannot be substantiated, the refund request may be denied.

---

## 22. Return Inspection and Approval

All returned products are subject to inspection upon receipt.

A return request may be denied if:

* The returned item differs from the item originally shipped.
* Components, accessories, manuals, or packaging are missing.
* The product shows signs of misuse or intentional damage.
* The product was altered or modified after delivery.
* Evidence submitted during the claim process is inaccurate or misleading.

The Company reserves the right to document inspection findings through photographs, videos, and internal records.

---

## 23. Rejected Returns

If a returned product does not satisfy the requirements of this Refund Policy, the refund request may be rejected.

In such cases:

* No refund will be issued.
* The returned item may be shipped back to the customer.
* The customer may be responsible for any return-shipping or re-shipping costs.
* If the customer does not arrange retrieval of the item within 30 days of notification, the Company may dispose of, recycle, liquidate, or otherwise handle the item at its discretion.

---

## 24. Product Substitution and Return Fraud

Customers must return the exact product originally received.

The following actions constitute refund fraud:

* Returning a different product.
* Returning counterfeit versions of the original product.
* Returning an empty box.
* Returning damaged items while claiming they arrived damaged.
* Removing components prior to return.
* Manipulating evidence submitted during the claim process.

If refund fraud is detected, the Company may:

* Deny the refund request.
* Permanently suspend the customer account.
* Restrict future purchases.
* Retain relevant evidence for dispute resolution.
* Pursue additional remedies permitted by applicable law.

---

## 25. Package Tampering and Delivery Disputes

Customers are encouraged to inspect packages immediately upon delivery.

Claims involving:

* Opened packages
* Missing contents
* Tampered packaging
* Empty-box deliveries

should be reported as soon as reasonably possible through the Company's AI Customer Support Agent.

The Company may require:

* Delivery photographs
* Unboxing videos
* Packaging images
* Carrier information

Failure to provide sufficient evidence may affect claim eligibility.

---

Where products are supplied by third-party sellers, the Company reserves the right to investigate seller conduct and product authenticity.

Verified violations may result in:

* Product delisting
* Seller suspension
* Seller termination
* Withholding of seller funds

The Company's investigation of a seller does not automatically guarantee a refund to the customer. Refund eligibility remains subject to this Refund Policy.
